Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers. Find out everything you need to know about building with us.

The cost of construction in Bangalore typically ranges from ₹1850 to ₹3000+ per square foot, depending on the package chosen. Our Construction Packages page provides a detailed breakdown of costs for Basic, Prime, and Luxury options, covering materials, finishes, and labor.

A typical residential home of around 1500-2500 sq.ft. usually takes between 8 to 12 months to complete from groundbreaking to handover. This timeline can vary based on the project's complexity, design approvals, and site conditions.

Our process is designed to be transparent and hassle-free. It includes: 1) Initial Consultation & Requirement Analysis, 2) Architectural Design & 3D Elevation, 3) Plan Approval & Legalities, 4) Construction & Project Management with regular updates, and 5) Handover & Post-completion support.

Yes, absolutely. This is a key part of our service. We have a dedicated team that handles all the necessary paperwork and liaison work for plan sanction from local authorities like BBMP and BDA, ensuring a smooth and legally compliant process for our clients.

Quality is our top priority at BuildCraft Constructions. We use only trusted brands for all core materials (as specified in our packages), conduct regular quality checks at every stage, and employ a team of experienced engineers and skilled labor to ensure the highest standards of workmanship.

Certainly. We are proud of the homes and commercial spaces we've built. You can browse our portfolio on our Projects page, which features detailed image galleries of our work across the city.

Our payment schedule is tied to construction milestones to ensure transparency and fairness. A typical schedule involves an initial booking advance, followed by payments upon completion of key stages like Foundation, Ground Floor Slab, First Floor Slab, Brickwork, Plastering, and finally, before Handover. A detailed schedule will be provided in your project agreement.

While we don't directly provide loans, we absolutely assist our clients in the process. We provide all the necessary technical documents, architectural drawings, and cost estimates required by banks and financial institutions for loan approval, making the process smoother for you.

Yes, we are flexible. We are happy to collaborate with your chosen architect and build according to your plans. Alternatively, if you don't have a plan, our in-house design team can work with you to create one from scratch based on your vision and requirements.

We stand by the quality of our work. BuildCraft Constructions offers a standard 1-year warranty covering any water seepage issues. Additionally, the building's core structure is guaranteed for 10 years against any structural defects. All branded materials used come with their respective manufacturer warranties.
