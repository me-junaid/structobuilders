Breaking Ground: Our New Commercial Project in Downtown

We’re excited to announce the launch of our latest project — a 12-story, mixed-use commercial development in the heart of downtown. This modern structure is designed with energy efficiency, durability, and cutting-edge technology in mind, making it a landmark addition to the city skyline.

Project Overview

The project includes:

  • 80,000 sq. ft. of office space
  • Retail outlets on the ground floor
  • Rooftop garden and green space
  • Underground parking for 100+ vehicles

Construction began earlier this month and is expected to be completed by Q4 2026.

Sustainable Construction at Its Core

We’re using:

  • Locally sourced, eco-friendly materials
  • High-efficiency HVAC and insulation systems
  • Smart lighting and automation for energy savings

This building aims for LEED Gold certification, aligning with our mission to build responsibly and sustainably.

Collaboration and Community Impact

We’re working closely with:

  • Local businesses
  • City planners
  • Skilled tradespeople from the community

The goal is not just to build another building — but to uplift the local economy and provide long-term benefits to the surrounding neighborhood.
